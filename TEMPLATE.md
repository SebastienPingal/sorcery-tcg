# Motivation

> What situation are we in? What business problem are we solving? What constraints exist?

> 

## Requirements

> What must the solution achieve? What are our key criteria?

> 

# Design Considerations/Options considered

> High overview of the technical problem. Either it is trivial and we just list options to consider. Or the problem is deeper, and we explore the problem into sub problems and options for each of them.

> 

Options considered:

> What approaches did we explore? For each option, document:

> 

> - How it works

> - Pros

> - Cons

> - (Optional) Cost/effort estimates

# Decision

> What did we choose and why? Clear statement of the chosen approach and rationale.

> 

# *[Optional] Implementation*

> How will this work in practice? Include:

> 

> - Key action items or next steps

> - Timeline if relevant

> - Risks and mitigation strategies

> - Success metrics if applicable
